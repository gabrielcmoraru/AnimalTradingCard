Lion

A male lion weighs about 500 pounds and grows to eight feet in length. It sounds impressive, but tigers are actually larger, reaching 850 pounds and 11 feet long.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Roar: Can be heard as far as 5 miles away
  • Speed: Can run for short distances at 50 mph and leap as far as 36 feet
  • Social Life: A lion may sleep up to 20 hours a day

Lions have strong, compact bodies and powerful forelegs, teeth, and jaws for pulling down and killing prey. Their coats are yellow-gold. Adult males have shaggy manes that range in color from blond to reddish-brown to black and also vary in length.
